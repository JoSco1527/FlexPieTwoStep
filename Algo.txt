Get a head count of how many people are eating, and what pizza is most popular between them
Gather all the ingredients you need to make your pizza of choice
From the silverware/utensil drawer, grab a spoon as well as can opener to be used for the pizza sauce
Open the can or jar of the sauce you use for your pizza, and roll out the premade pizza dough (to desired size)
Add a copious amount of sauce to the pizza dough, then cheese and whatever toppings you wish
Throw the pizza in the oven at 425 for 15 mins
Pull out the pizza and check bottom of crust to ensure its cooked thoroughly
Cut pizza and enjoy!
